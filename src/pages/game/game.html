HOW TO PLAY

The main goal of the game is to perform arithmetics operations for complex fractions as soon as possible.

There are 4 kinds of arithmetics operations for complex fractions: to double, to tripple, to find one-half, to find one-third. One of these operations is selected randomly - you should perform it during the game. It is indicated in the top of the window.

Under the operation name there is a row of complex fractions which were randomly generated for this game. You should perform the operation for every complex fraction.

To compose an answer you should use circle parts which show parts of complex fractions. Try to drag some circles into the answer block while you get the correct answer. Please, don't forget to cut fractions.

You should click the button to submit your answer. If the answer is correct the button will be green but if the answer is wrong the button will be red.

When you perform the operation for every complex fractions the game will be finished and you will see your score - the time while you perform operations. If you play twice the game will show the result of your speed: the game you have played was going faster or slower as prevous one, the difference between them.

Good luck!
